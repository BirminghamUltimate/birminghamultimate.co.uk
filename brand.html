The Birmingham Ultimate Brand

This serves as a guide on how to present Birmingham Ultimate's brand identity.

The Bull Icon

The Bull Icon was created to honour the rich history and culture of Birmingham, as well as the strength and determination of our players.

It was designed in 2025 by Force Ultimate, the provider of our team kits and branding.

The Word Marque

The Word Marque represents the Birmingham Ultimate name and is a key element of our brand identity.
